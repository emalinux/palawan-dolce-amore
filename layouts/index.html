Supporting tribal communities in Palawan

Our services include health care support, food and shelter provision, and assistance in developing a sense of pride amongst the tribe, which helps build courage and prevent discrimination.

Palawan “Dolce Amore” Tribe Association is a not-for-profit organisation which helps tribal and indigenous people in need and provides opportunities for the benefit of our communities.

How your help makes a difference

Health & care

Basic medical support, medicines and transport so that families in remote areas can reach hospitals and clinics.

Food & shelter

Emergency food, safe places to stay and essential items for children and elders in the tribe.

Dignity & education

School materials, community activities and projects that help build confidence and reduce discrimination.
